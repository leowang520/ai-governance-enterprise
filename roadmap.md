# Roadmap – Enterprise AI Governance

## Phase 1: Foundation (2025)
- Define AI governance framework
- Establish enterprise AI usage policies
- Align with EU AI Act and ISO/IEC 42001

## Phase 2: Expansion (2026)
- Integrate AI risk management into enterprise processes
- Deploy AI-enabled IT operations (Copilot, automation)
- Implement Zero Trust + AI security monitoring

## Phase 3: Optimization (2027+)
- AI-driven risk quantification
- Cross-region joint defense ecosystem
- Continuous compliance and governance automation
